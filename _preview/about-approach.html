How a project actually works

  1. 01

    Discovery

    We talk before I write a single line of code. I want to understand the real problem — not just the requirements document.

  2. 02

    Proposal

    A clear scope, timeline, and cost. You know exactly what you're getting before we start. No vague estimates, no hidden assumptions.

  3. 03

    Build

    You see progress as it happens. Regular check-ins, working software early, and a direct line to me throughout.

  4. 04

    Launch

    Careful deployment, thorough testing, and a handover that makes sense. I'm still available after go-live, not just before it.

  5. 05

    After

    Bugs get fixed. Questions get answered. If something needs to change six months from now, I'm a message away.
