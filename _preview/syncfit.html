Up and Running in Minutes

1

Download SyncFit

Get SyncFit from the App Store and start your 7-day free trial.

2

Connect Fitbit

Sign in with your Fitbit account. SyncFit will request read access to your health data.

3

Grant Apple Health Access

Choose which metrics SyncFit can write to Apple Health. You stay in full control.

4

Sync Starts Automatically

SyncFit syncs in the background daily. Your data appears in Apple Health and any connected app.

🔒

Your Data Stays Yours

SyncFit connects directly to Fitbit's official API using OAuth. Your Fitbit credentials are never stored by SyncFit — authentication is handled entirely by Fitbit. Health data is written locally to Apple Health on your device and never transmitted to our servers. No ads, no data brokering, no tracking.

✓ Requires Fitbit account & Apple Health access
